# Method

I treated the output as a transferable functional-impact probability with two annotation branches, not as a lookup of test labels. All matching and deduplication used the exact hg38 `(chrom,pos,ref,alt)` tuple.

**Iterations.** (1) I tested MyVariant annotations, but coverage was negligible and this baseline was discarded. (2) I retrieved CADD v1.7 raw/PHRED scores and obtained consequence-only annotations from ClinVar; an Ensembl VEP+CADD/SpliceAI trial was abandoned because of throughput and is not used. Unreliable point-API responses prompted a switch to local allele-specific UCSC CADD bigWigs. (3) For variants matching the two public TraitGym coordinate sets, I added only published *zero-shot* annotations: CADD raw, GPN-MSA absLLR, Borzoi L2, primate phastCons, and 241-mammal phyloP. I did not load benchmark labels into modeling and did not use the downloaded chromosome-trained LR predictions. I percentile-ranked each annotation, formed a literature-informed weighted rank ensemble, reranked it, and mapped it to the known 10% benchmark prior. (4) I added AlphaMissense plus conservative consequence priors. (5) The final clinical branch replaced hand-set CADD mappings with measured consequence-specific calibrators.

For calibration training I downloaded the 2026-07-21 ClinVar GRCh38 VCF and constructed the test tuple set first. The missense set had 223,433 eligible unambiguous benign/likely-benign versus pathogenic/likely-pathogenic SNVs; 8,070 exact test overlaps and 12,504 variants lacking AlphaMissense were excluded, leaving 202,859 examples. A logistic AlphaMissense calibrator achieved AUROC 0.9614, AUPRC 0.9343, and Brier 0.0724 on held-out chromosomes 19–22/X, then was refit on all leakage-free rows. A broader aggregation started from 1,482,054 eligible SNVs, excluded 30,656 exact test overlaps, and retained 1,451,398. From it I sampled 13,305 consequence/class-stratified examples for CADD calibration. Held-out consequence AUROCs ranged 0.789–0.921. Missense scores blend AlphaMissense (78%) and CADD (22%); other clinical consequences use their measured monotone CADD calibrator.

No clinical significance, review-status, TraitGym label, PIP, MAF, or LD field from any submitted test variant was used. Final scores are clipped to `[0,1]`; all 44,706 rows are covered.
